about us


Puthalath Designs is a well-organized and fully-fledged Architecture and interior design company in Kasaragod offering comprehensive Architecture and interior design solutions. We make use of the latest design technologies and work with a commitment to bring out the best possible result. Our well-planned Architecture and interior designs process include space planning, layout plan 2D drawings, 3D views, electrical & plumbing layout planning, Custom made furniture and revisions towards the final result to the complete satisfaction of the client. Founded in 2017, Puthalath Designs has built a versatile crew of architects, interior designers, draftsmen, 3D visualizers, site supervisors, administrators, project managers, skilled and trained workers.
